Welcome TO The SCP FOUNDATION

What is the SCP Foundation?

The SCP Foundation is a fictional, internet-based organization that is presented as a secret global agency tasked with discovering, containing, and studying anomalous entities, objects, and phenomena.

What are SCPs?

SCPs, or "Special Containment Procedures," are the entries that document these anomalous entities, objects, and phenomena, and the procedures used to contain them.

This the simple introduction for all those SCPs

The SCP Foundation contains a wide array of anomalous objects, each with unique and often dangerous properties. SCP-002, a tumorous, fleshy growth resembling a low-rent apartment, "furnishes" itself using organic matter from living humans, making it a high-priority containment object. SCP-003 consists of two components: SCP-003-1, a sentient, motherboard-like structure made of biological materials, and SCP-003-2, a stone tablet that emits radiation to inhibit SCP-003-1's exponential growth. SCP-004 is an old wooden barn door accompanied by twelve rusted keys, each unlocking the door to a different dimensionâ€”most of which are lethally hostile to humans. SCP-005 is an ornate key capable of unlocking any lock, mechanical or digital, though its sentient nature raises questions about its origins and limitations. Finally, SCP-006 is a small spring with water that possesses extraordinary regenerative properties, capable of repairing DNA damage and enhancing the immune system to near-invulnerability. Together, these SCPs represent the Foundation's mission to secure, contain, and protect humanity from the unknown.
